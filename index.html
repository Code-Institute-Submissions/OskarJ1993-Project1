Welcome to LOGO Resturant & Bar

A taste of Sweden

We are excited to offer you a taste of traditional Swedish cuisine in a cozy and inviting atmosphere. Our menu features a variety of classic dishes made from the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. From hearty meatballs and lingonberry sauce, to fresh seafood and homemade breads, we have something for everyone to enjoy. Our chefs are dedicated to preserving the authentic flavors of Sweden while also experimenting with new and innovative dishes. Come and experience the warmth and comfort of a traditional Swedish meal with us. We look forward to serving you!
